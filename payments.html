Payment Policy

Our payment policy outlines the terms and conditions for making payments on our website:

1. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment gateways. Your credit/debit card information is not stored on our servers, ensuring your financial data remains safe.

2. Payment Confirmation

Once your payment is successfully processed, you will receive a confirmation email containing the details of your transaction.

3. Refund Policy

We have a transparent refund policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days of the purchase date. Please refer to our Refund Policy page for more information.

4. Failed Payments

If a payment fails for any reason, please ensure that your payment information is correct and try again. If the issue persists, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.

5. Currency and Taxes

All prices and transactions are conducted in the local currency (USD). Prices are inclusive of all applicable taxes and fees, unless stated otherwise.

6. Payment Security

Your payment security is of utmost importance to us. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information during transmission.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our payment methods or policy, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
